Our Doctrine

What We Believe

Our faith is rooted in the Holy Scriptures, centered on Jesus Christ, empowered by the Holy Spirit, and committed to the advancement of God's Kingdom among the nations.

Biblical Foundation

Essential Doctrinal Truths

Christ Impact Ministries International holds firmly to biblical truth as revealed in Scripture and affirms the foundational doctrines of the Christian faith.

Doctrine 01

The Bible

We believe the Holy Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. Scripture is God's revealed truth for doctrine, correction, instruction, and spiritual growth.

2 Timothy 3:16 • 2 Peter 1:20–21

Doctrine 02

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three Persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, equal in divinity, power, glory, and eternal nature.

Matthew 28:19

Doctrine 03

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is fully God and fully man, conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the Virgin Mary, lived a sinless life, died for our sins, rose bodily from the grave, ascended to the Father, and will return again in glory.

John 1:1,14 • 1 Corinthians 15:3–4 • Acts 1:11

Doctrine 04

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, apart from works. Regeneration through the Holy Spirit is essential for every believer.

Ephesians 2:8–9 • Titus 3:5

Doctrine 05

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present-day ministry of the Holy Spirit, including conviction, regeneration, indwelling, empowerment, spiritual gifts, baptism, and service for Kingdom ministry.

Acts 1:8 • 1 Corinthians 12 • Romans 12

Doctrine 06

Baptism & Communion

We believe in water baptism by immersion as an act of obedience to Christ and in the Lord's Supper as a sacred remembrance of His death and sacrifice for humanity.

Matthew 28:19 • 1 Corinthians 11:23–26

Doctrine 07

Healing & Deliverance

We believe divine healing and deliverance are provided through Christ's atonement and remain an active expression of the Gospel ministry through faith and the power of God.

Isaiah 53:4–5 • Mark 16:17–18 • James 5:14–16

Doctrine 08

The Second Coming

We believe in the personal and visible return of Jesus Christ, the resurrection of the dead, eternal life for believers, and final judgment.

1 Thessalonians 4:16–17 • Revelation 20–21

Doctrine 09

The Great Commission

We believe every believer is called to preach the Gospel, make disciples, and demonstrate the Kingdom of God through faithful witness and service.

Matthew 28:18–20 • Mark 16:15–18
