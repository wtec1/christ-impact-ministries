Discipleship & Growth

Training & Discipleship

We believe every believer is called to spiritual maturity, Kingdom service, and leadership growth. Our discipleship pipeline equips believers step-by-step to fulfill God's purpose.

Growth Pathway

The CIMI Training Pipeline

Every believer is encouraged to progress through our discipleship journey designed to build spiritual maturity, biblical knowledge, leadership capacity, and missionary impact.

Step 01

Foundation

Understanding salvation, spiritual growth, prayer, faith, biblical doctrine, and Christian living.

Step 02

Witness

Equipping believers to share the Gospel, evangelize, disciple others, and serve in ministry.

Step 03

Leadership

Developing spiritual leadership, mentorship, ministry oversight, and Kingdom responsibility.

Step 04

Sending

Preparing believers for missions, church planting, Kingdom expansion, and ministry deployment.

Stage One

Foundation Track

This foundational stage introduces believers to the essentials of Christian faith and spiritual growth.

  • • Salvation & assurance
  • • Prayer & devotion
  • • Biblical foundations
  • • Christian lifestyle
  • • Holy Spirit basics

Duration: 6–8 Weeks

Requirements: Open to all believers

Register

Stage Two

Witness Track

Believers are equipped to boldly share Christ and engage in ministry and evangelism.

  • • Personal evangelism
  • • Gospel presentation
  • • Soul winning
  • • Discipleship methods
  • • Ministry service

Duration: 8–10 Weeks

Requirements: Completion of Foundation Track

Register

Stage Three

Leadership Track

This stage develops future ministry leaders through leadership training, mentoring, and Kingdom responsibility.

  • • Leadership principles
  • • Ministry oversight
  • • Mentorship
  • • Team management
  • • Biblical leadership

Duration: 12 Weeks

Requirements: Completion of Witness Track

Register

Stage Four

Sending Track

Preparing believers for missions, church planting, Kingdom deployment, and ministry expansion.

  • • Missions preparation
  • • Church planting vision
  • • Kingdom assignment
  • • Leadership deployment
  • • Spiritual maturity

Duration: 8–12 Weeks

Requirements: Completion of Leadership Track
