Join CIMI

Become part of a Christ-centered community committed to discipleship, Kingdom impact, worship, evangelism, prayer, and spiritual growth.

Membership

Membership Types

01

Active Member

For believers committed to worship attendance, discipleship, church service, and spiritual growth within the local church family.

  • • Local church participation
  • • Ministry involvement
  • • Spiritual growth pathway
  • • Leadership development

02

Associate Member

For believers connected to the ministry but living in another city, church region, or international location.

  • • Online discipleship
  • • Events participation
  • • Prayer partnership
  • • Ministry support

03

Ministry Partner

For individuals committed to supporting the mission through prayer, giving, missions, and Kingdom advancement.

  • • Prayer support
  • • Financial partnership
  • • Missions support
  • • Outreach participation
