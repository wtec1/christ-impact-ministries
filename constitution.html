Governance

Apostolic Oversight & Executive Governance

CIMI operates under the Apostolic and Prophetic Oversight Council (APOC), responsible for doctrinal purity, mission approval, leadership appointments, and strategic direction.

The Executive Leadership Team (ELT) manages daily ministry operations, missions, discipleship, media, finances, and implementation of ministry vision.

Financial Stewardship

Financial Accountability

  • • Tithes, offerings, donations, and grants support ministry operations.
  • • Proper books and financial reporting are maintained.
  • • Annual audit or internal review ensures accountability.
  • • Ministry income is used solely for religious and mission purposes.
  • • Financial misuse is prohibited.
