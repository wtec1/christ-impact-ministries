About Christ Impact Ministries

A ministry committed to preaching Christ Jesus to the nations, demonstrating God’s Kingdom in power and the Holy Spirit.

Who We Are

Raising A Generation For Kingdom Impact

Christ Impact Ministries International (CIMI) exists to preach Christ Jesus to the nations, demonstrate the power of God's Kingdom, and raise Christ-centered generations grounded in biblical truth.

Founded upon the authority of Scripture and the Lordship of Jesus Christ, CIMI seeks to transform lives through evangelism, discipleship, prayer, healing, missions, and spiritual empowerment.
